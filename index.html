About

Make-a-Ton, presented by CITTIC, invites innovative solutions to tackle issues faced by the world today. This 24 hour open hackathon provides a platform for University students as well as professionals from the industry, to pitch their ideas in front of an eminent panel of judges. Projects and ideas can be presented even if they lack a full fledged business plan, as Make-a-Ton focusses on bringing out passionate innovators. Solutions can be provided to problems that fall under five different categories. Grand prizes await the winners!

Whats up for grabs?

Prizes and Goodies
Opportunity to take home prizes and swags worth 1.5Lac.
Incubation Opportunities
Connect with people in the industry while you build your product to grab incubation offers.
Get Mentored
We've invited experts from the industry to help you build better.
Holistic Experience
Meet students from all over India and be exposed to a wide range of ideas.
